Frequently Asked Questions

How can I buy ticket on RMR Travels?

The process is very easy. Go to the main page of RMR Travels and enter the source and destination locations. Enter the date of travle and search the bus. Now choose the bus and enter your details and book the ticket.

Can I cancel my bus ticket online?

Yes, you can, provided that the bus operator supports it as not all operators offer a cacnellation option when you book your tickets with them.

Can I change the date of my journey after I have booked the ticket?

Yes, you can. You can go to the modify details option and change your booking details.
